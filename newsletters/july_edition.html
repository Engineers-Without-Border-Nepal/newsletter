Immersive Study Tour 2025: A Journey of Learning, Collaboration, and Community

The Immersive Study Tour 2025, organized by Engineers Without Borders Nepal in collaboration with IngOG+, brought together changemakers from Nepal, Switzerland, and Singapore to explore rural resilience, sustainability, and intercultural exchange. From hands-on community engagement in Dhading to collaborative project ideation, the tour laid the foundation for lasting impact and future global partnerships.

Innovation in the Terai: How iLab Simara Sparked Creativity and Courage

From June 23–27, iLab Simara turned a school in the Terai into a vibrant hub of creativity and problem-solving. Students explored local issues, built hands-on solutions, and embraced innovation—from 3D printing to game design—with growing confidence. Funded and supported by WorldLink, the program didn’t just teach tools; it nurtured mindsets, sparked ownership, and forged lasting bonds between mentors, learners, and their communities.

Empowering Young Innovators: iJatra at Shrigneri School

EWB Nepal, in collaboration with Karyashala, hosted iJatra at Shrigneri School — a showcase of student-led creativity and entrepreneurship. From handmade goods to cultural foods and card games, the event celebrated innovation, sustainability, and the power of youth-driven change.

EWBN’s WASH Assessment at Shree Roshi Khola Secondary School

EWBN recently conducted a WASH assessment at Roshi Secondary School in Kavre, focusing on drinking water, sanitation, and infrastructure. After engaging with school officials and surveying facilities, the team proposed initial solutions to help address the school’s most pressing challenges.

EWB Nepal and DHBW Stuttgart Wrap Up 9-Week Global Innovation Program

EWB Nepal and DHBW Stuttgart successfully concluded a 9-week virtual academic program that united 40 students from Nepal and Germany in cross-cultural teams to tackle real-world challenges. From drone solutions for mountain cleanup to LLMs for local use, the initiative fostered global collaboration, innovation, and hands-on learning—laying the groundwork for future international academic exchanges.

Disaster Preparedness Session Bridges Science, Youth, and Community Action

On June 23, EWB Nepal, with IAAS Nepal and EWBN Pulchowk Chapter, hosted a hybrid Disaster Preparedness Session featuring Dr. Basanta Raj Adhikari and Mr. Sagar Koirala. The event focused on monsoon risks, cascading hazards, and resilience, drawing strong participation.
