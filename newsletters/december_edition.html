Key Highlights

Kapil Dev Regmi appointed as President of EWB International

We are proud to announce that Mr. Kapil Dev Regmi, Founder and President of EWB Nepal, has been appointed President of EWB International. His visionary leadership marks a major milestone for Nepal, strengthening global collaboration and advancing sustainable engineering impact worldwide.

Skanda Rimal appointed as new General Manager of EWBN

We are pleased to announce the appointment of Skanda Rimal as General Manager of Engineers Without Borders Nepal. With expertise in sustainable design and community-driven engineering, she brings strong leadership to advance EWBN’s mission of impactful, resilient, and sustainable development initiatives.

Strengthening and Broadening Impact with Worldlink: MoU signed with Worldlink

EWBN has signed an MoU with WorldLink to expand its flagship iLab project to Koshi and Gandaki Provinces in FY 2082/83. This partnership will promote innovation-driven education, skill development, and community-based learning, empowering students and strengthening local innovation ecosystems.

Project Updates

Empowering the Future Changemakers: iLab at Jorpati

EWBN launched an iLab at Nava Sunthakan Academy, actively engaging Grade 8–9 students in hands-on projects focused on innovation, problem-solving, teamwork, and sustainable solutions using local resources.

Learn more

Listening Back to the Community: Dhading Post-Assessment Visit

EWB Nepal conducted a post-assessment visit to Dhading, reviewing education, infrastructure, and livelihoods while identifying priorities for sustainable development, community empowerment, and future project planning.

Learn more

A celebration of student innovation: i-Jatra at Darchula

i-JATRA 2082, held on 7th November at Shree Malikarjun Secondary School, brought together students, educators, and the community. Student-led stalls showcased creative projects, 3D prototypes, and local products, fostering entrepreneurship, teamwork, and hands-on learning while engaging over 4,000 visitors in a vibrant educational festival.
