EWB Nepal’s Annual General Meeting 2025: From Ideas to Impact

Engineers Without Borders Nepal (EWBN) held its Annual General Meeting on August 12, celebrating a year of community-driven engineering, youth engagement, and sustainable impact. Members reflected on achievements, shared ideas to strengthen programs, and renewed their commitment to advancing hope and empowerment across Nepal.

EWB Nepal Strengthens Global Ties with MoUs with EWB UK and EWB Sweden

Engineers Without Borders Nepal (EWB-N) formed new partnerships with EWB UK and EWB Sweden, enhancing global collaboration and sustainable engineering. An MoU with EWB UK promotes shared learning and community projects, enabling Nepali students to join the Engineering for People Design Challenge. Leaders hailed it as a model for mutual growth and impact.

Mr. Kapil Dev Regmi’s Europe Visit: Strengthening Global Partnerships for Sustainable Engineering

This September, Mr. Kapil Dev Regmi, President of EWB Nepal and President-Elect of EWB International, visited several European countries to strengthen global partnerships. His trip included signing an MoU with EWB UK, meetings with EWB Sweden, and participation in the SEED and stars Symposia, promoting collaboration in sustainability and innovation.

EWB Nepal Welcomes Global Mentors Jonas Buol and Steven L. Bosiljevac

EWB Nepal warmly welcomes two new global mentors, Mr. Jonas Buol and Mr. Steven L. Bosiljevac. With vast international experience in sustainability, education, and entrepreneurship, they will mentor the team, foster global collaborations, and provide strategic guidance to strengthen EWB Nepal’s mission of sustainable engineering, innovation, and community-driven impact.

EWBN and CloudFactory Forge Partnership for Community-driven Innovation

EWBN has signed a Memorandum of Understanding with CloudFactory to foster talent development, collaboration on AI for social impact, and community-driven initiatives.
